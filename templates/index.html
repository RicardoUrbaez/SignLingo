How It Works

Four steps from hand gesture to text and speech.

1

Capture Gesture

The webcam captures your hand gesture in real time via the live video feed.

2

Detect Landmarks

MediaPipe extracts 21 hand landmark coordinates from each captured frame.

3

Classify Sign

A trained Random Forest model classifies the normalized landmarks into the corresponding ASL sign.

4

Output Text & Speech

The recognized sign displays as text and can be spoken aloud via the Speak button.
